## Context

SPEC-11 R6 mandates that `merge()` in the merge module is annotated with `#[tracing::instrument]`. The merge phase is a critical step in the grid cycle where partitions are reunited and border redexes are resolved. The span fields `partition_count` and `border_redexes` are essential for understanding merge complexity and diagnosing performance in multi-round reductions.
